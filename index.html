Photography Competition 2024 - Judging

Judging Criteria

  1. Creativity and Originality:

    Creativity and originality refer to the photographer's ability to capture a scene or subject in a unique and innovative way. It involves choosing interesting perspectives, experimenting with composition, and bringing a fresh approach to commonly photographed subjects.

  2. Technical Excellence:

    Technical excellence is about the photographer's proficiency in handling the camera, understanding lighting, and utilizing editing tools effectively. It includes aspects like focus, exposure, sharpness, and post-processing skills, all contributing to the overall technical quality of the photograph.

  3. Composition:

    Composition involves arranging visual elements within the frame to create a visually appealing and balanced image.

  4. Artistic Merit:

    Artistic merit is tied to the aesthetic quality and meaningfulness of the image. It involves the photographer's ability to convey a story, mood, or concept through their work. A photograph with artistic merit goes beyond technical aspects to make a broader impact on the viewer.

  5. Emotional Impact:

    Emotional impact relates to the ability of an image to evoke emotions in the viewer. Whether it's capturing a fleeting moment of joy, the serenity of a landscape, or the intensity of a portrait, a photograph with emotional impact resonates with the audience on a personal and emotional level.
